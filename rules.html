Club Rules

Respect the World

These rules help everyone enjoy Minecraft Lunchtime Club and keep the world safe, fair and fun.

Back to Challenges

Core Rules

1. No griefing

Do not destroy, damage or change another student’s build without permission.

2. Build positively

Add to the world in a way that improves it. Make your work thoughtful and deliberate.

3. Stay on task

Choose a challenge and work towards completing it. Free build should still have a purpose.

4. Respect chat

Use kind, sensible language. No spam, arguments or unhelpful comments.

5. Ask before joining

Do not take over another player’s area. Ask before helping or adding to someone else’s build.

6. Save evidence

Take screenshots or be ready to show your work at the end of the club session.

Good club behaviour looks like...

  • sharing ideas without bossing others around
  • helping someone who is stuck
  • testing and improving your own build
  • using Minecraft Education tools responsibly
  • letting others be proud of their work

Finished early?

Improve your build, add labels, take better screenshots, help someone else, or attempt the stretch challenge.
