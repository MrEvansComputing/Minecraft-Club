Challenge outline

  1. Plan the maze layout.
  2. Build walls, paths and dead ends.
  3. Add a clear start and finish.
  4. Test the maze yourself.
  5. Ask another player to try it and improve unclear parts.
Evidence: Screenshot from above or several screenshots showing the maze.

Stretch challenge

Add themed rooms, hidden clues or a reward chest at the finish.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
