Castle Defence Challenge

Build a small castle or fortress designed to protect the people inside.

StrategyBuildingDefence

Challenge outline

  1. Choose a location with defensive potential.
  2. Build walls, entrance and internal areas.
  3. Add defensive features.
  4. Make the castle functional, not just decorative.
  5. Prepare to explain how your castle protects people.
Evidence: Screenshot of the full castle and one defence feature.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Create a siege test: explain how attackers might try to get in and how your design stops them.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
