Food Farm Challenge

Create a useful food farm that could keep players fed in survival mode.

Challenge outline

  1. Choose a flat or natural area for the farm.
  2. Build crop areas or animal pens.
  3. Add water, fences, gates, lighting and paths.
  4. Organise the farm so it is easy to use.
  5. Take a screenshot showing the full farm.
Evidence: Screenshot of the whole farm and one useful feature.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Add a storage chest area, composting area or farmer’s hut.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
