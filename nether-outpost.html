Challenge outline

  1. Create or choose a Nether-themed area.
  2. Build a protected outpost.
  3. Add storage, lighting and safe routes.
  4. Add warning signs or defensive features.
  5. Take screenshots showing how it protects players.
Evidence: Screenshot of the outpost and safety features.

Stretch challenge

Create a portal room linked to the outpost with a secure entrance.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
