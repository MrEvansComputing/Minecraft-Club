Redstone Logic Gate Challenge

Build a simple redstone logic gate, such as AND, OR or NOT, and show how it works.

RedstoneComputer ScienceLogic
Previous Next Mission Select

Challenge outline

  1. Choose one logic gate: AND, OR or NOT.
  2. Build the input switches.
  3. Connect the redstone circuit to an output lamp or door.
  4. Test all possible input combinations.
  5. Add signs showing the rule.
Evidence: Screenshot showing inputs, circuit and output.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Build two different gates next to each other and compare how they work.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
