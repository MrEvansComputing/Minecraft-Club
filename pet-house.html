Minecraft Pet House Challenge

Build a safe and comfortable home for a Minecraft animal or pet.

CreativeAnimalsBuilding
Previous Next Mission Select

Challenge outline

  1. Choose an animal or pet.
  2. Build a home that suits that animal.
  3. Add food, water, bedding or play areas.
  4. Decorate the area to make it welcoming.
  5. Take a screenshot of your finished pet house.
Evidence: Screenshot of the pet house and animal area.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Create a sign explaining the pet’s name, favourite food and special feature.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
