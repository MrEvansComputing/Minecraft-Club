Minecraft Theme Park Ride Challenge

Build a Minecraft theme park ride or attraction that visitors would want to try.

MinecartsDesignAttraction
Previous Next Mission Select

Challenge outline

  1. Choose a ride idea, such as roller coaster, haunted mine, boat ride, drop tower illusion or scenic tour.
  2. Build the entrance and queue area.
  3. Create the main ride experience.
  4. Add decoration and signs so it has a clear theme.
  5. Test the ride from start to finish.
Evidence: Screenshot of the entrance and ride experience.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Add a gift shop, photo point or scoreboard at the end of the ride.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
