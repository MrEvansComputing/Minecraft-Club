Starter Shelter Challenge

Build a safe starter shelter that would help a player survive their first night.

SurvivalBuildingDesign
Previous Next Mission Select

Challenge outline

  1. Open Minecraft Education and create or join the club world.
  2. Choose a suitable location for a starter shelter.
  3. Build a shelter with walls, a roof, lighting and an entrance.
  4. Add at least three useful survival features.
  5. Take a screenshot when your build is finished.
Evidence: Screenshot of the outside and inside of your shelter.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Add a small labelled area outside the shelter showing where food, mining, storage and defence would happen.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
