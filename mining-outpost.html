Mining Outpost Challenge

Build a small mining outpost that helps players prepare for underground exploration.

Challenge outline

  1. Choose or create a mine entrance.
  2. Build a small outpost near the entrance.
  3. Add storage, crafting and safety features.
  4. Light the area clearly.
  5. Take a screenshot showing the mine entrance and outpost.
Evidence: Screenshot of the mine entrance and outpost.

Stretch challenge

Add a minecart loading area or labelled tunnel system.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
