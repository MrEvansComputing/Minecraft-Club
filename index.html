How this website works

This website gives you the challenge outline. You will still need to open Minecraft Education to complete the challenge.

  • Pick a category or challenge.
  • Read the brief and checklist.
  • Build or complete the task in Minecraft Education.
  • Take screenshots or be ready to show your work.
