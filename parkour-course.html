Parkour Course Challenge

Design a parkour course that is fun, fair and possible to complete.

Challenge outline

  1. Build a clear start point and finish point.
  2. Create a sequence of jumps or movement challenges.
  3. Test your course repeatedly to check it is possible.
  4. Add signs, checkpoints or decoration.
  5. Ask someone else to test it and give feedback.
Evidence: Screenshot of the full course and one action screenshot.

Stretch challenge

Add difficulty routes: easy path, medium path and expert path.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
