Village Upgrade Challenge

Improve a village so it becomes safer, clearer and more useful for the villagers.

CommunityPlanningCreative
Previous Next Mission Select

Challenge outline

  1. Find or create a village area in Minecraft Education.
  2. Identify what the village needs to improve.
  3. Add useful buildings, paths, lighting or safety features.
  4. Make sure your improvements fit the village style.
  5. Take screenshots showing before/after or different areas.
Evidence: Screenshots showing your improved village areas.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Create signs explaining what each new area is for and how it helps the village.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
