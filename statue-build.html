Giant Statue Challenge

Build a giant statue of a mob, player, item or original character.

CreativeScaleDesign
Previous Next Mission Select

Challenge outline

  1. Choose the statue subject.
  2. Plan the basic shape before adding detail.
  3. Build the main body using large block sections.
  4. Add colours, details and a display base.
  5. Take screenshots from the front and side.
Evidence: Front and side screenshots of the statue.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Add a plaque, lighting and a small viewing platform.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
