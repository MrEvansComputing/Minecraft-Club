Challenge outline

  1. Choose a theme, such as city, castle kingdom, theme park, space base, underwater world or fantasy island.
  2. Plan the different areas of the build.
  3. Divide responsibilities if working with others.
  4. Build with a consistent style.
  5. Prepare a final tour or screenshot showcase.
Evidence: Screenshots from multiple viewpoints or a short tour.

Success checklist

Stretch challenge

Create a visitor route with signs, landmarks and hidden secrets to discover.

Before moving on

  • Check every item on the success checklist.
  • Take your screenshot or prepare to show your build.
  • Make one improvement to make your build clearer, safer, more creative or more detailed.
